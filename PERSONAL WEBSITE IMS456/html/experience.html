Short Trip


It was the last semester break of my diploma. Me and my other friends decided to go to short trip at Rumah Ndoi, Baling, Kedah.
We stay for 2days and 1night but for me it was very best moment or experience to me. After that, we also went to Baling Hotspring.
For us, enjoying hotspring water at night will heal all of the stress. The next day, we wake up early to go the
"Tiptop Corner" which is they sell "Roti Canai" in variety of choices for breakfast. Customers can choose to add on cheese or not.
The price is very cheap with that kind of taste. There are many outside people come to breakfast at this stall.


This is the best experience with my friends because, all of us not come from Kedah and it more difficult to meet them because of this pandemic.
But all of us manage to spend a little quality time and still make sure we follow all the SOP to avoid spreading or infected by Covid-19.
Even it was the short trip but all of us still enjoying the moment. We only have a few months before this last semester end,
so we manage to spend the time before we all split up and follow each other's choices whether to work or continue studying.



I really recommend to those who want to do camping thing at Rumah Ndoi because it is the right place for people that love the nature.
All of us just sleep in the tent that can fit 2 people.It was very cool moment for me cause need to fight with some insect like mosquito and ant.
At the night we just chilling at the place by playing some games, story time and drinking coffee. The vibe very suit for people to chill with family and friends.
